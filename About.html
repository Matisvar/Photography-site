About Me

DhineshKumar is a photographer based in Karur, India, who in recent years has taken a place among the foremost practitioners in the Scandinavian outdoor and adventure genre. With a background in Electronics and Communication engineering, Dhinesh started his career working with Mobile photography and has several years of experience with creative retouching, layout and graphic design. In combination with a great passion for the outdoors, Dhinesh thrives working in wild and challenging environments, living to tell stories through still photography and motion pictures with the goal to strengthen our connection to the natural world
